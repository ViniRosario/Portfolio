# **App Name**: Vini's GlitchFolio

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Content Display: Render all portfolio sections ('About Me', 'Skills', 'Tools', 'Video Gallery', 'Contact') using a 'digital zine' and 'urban collage' aesthetic, breaking traditional layouts and integrating content organically.
- Aggressive Section Transitions: Implement dramatic and abrupt visual effects such as 'glitch', 'pixel explosion', or 'digital paper tear' for navigation between different portfolio sections.
- Generative Chaotic Skills Display: Utilize an AI tool to dynamically arrange and style skills in a chaotic, typographic explosion, simulating words cut from magazines with varying sizes, weights, and rotations, moving away from conventional lists.
- Interactive Micro-animations & Hover Effects: Integrate constant micro-animations to maintain a 'live' design with an analog noise feel, and aggressive, distorted visual reactions for elements on hover.
- Collage-Style Tools & Stack: Present software icons and names as 'stickers' or 'taped pieces' with overlay effects, incorporating subtle parallax scrolling and a 'paper tear' visual effect on interaction.
- Unconventional Video Gallery: Display a primary video in focus, surrounded by floating 'thumbnail' videos integrated into the collage design, which expand with a visual 'explosion' effect when clicked.
- Energetic Contact Section: Maintain the site's high energy in the contact section with links to social media icons and a contact form featuring subtle glitch or old TV noise effects on interaction.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: A very dark, desaturated reddish-purple (#22141F) to create depth and contrast, setting an unconventional, nocturnal tone.
- Primary accent color: A vibrant, bold fuchsia (#F40FC0) for main calls to action and emphasis, conveying strong energy and a rebellious spirit, reminiscent of neon urban art.
- Secondary accent color: A deep, electrifying purple (#9214CC) providing a complementary vivid contrast, analogous to the primary while offering visual diversity.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its computerized, techy, and expressive feel, perfect for bold, attention-grabbing titles.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) to ensure high readability and a modern, objective contrast for supporting text, while complementing the headline's avant-garde style.
- Utilize custom-designed, 'handmade' graphic elements and 'collage' style icons resembling cut-outs or adhesive-backed stickers to emphasize imperfection and an underground aesthetic.
- Employ an asymmetrical, non-grid-based layout with fragmented and overlapping elements, mimicking the 'zine' and 'urban collage' aesthetic for a visually dynamic and surprising user experience.
- Integrate aggressive, non-smooth transition effects such as glitches, pixel bursts, and digital paper tears between sections. Elements should feature dramatic distortion, color shifts, and constant micro-animations simulating analog noise upon interaction or hover.
